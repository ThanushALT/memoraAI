Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Memora

How do I get started with Memora?

Getting started is easy! Just sign up for a free 7-day trial, and you'll be guided through the process of creating your first journal entry. You can start with a simple reflection, or use one of our curated prompts to inspire your writing.

What features are included in the $2/month plan?

Our $2/month plan includes everything you need for a complete journaling experience: unlimited entries, AI-powered insights, mood tracking, custom prompts, full entry history, cross-device syncing, and premium customer support.

Is my journal data private and secure?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All journal entries are encrypted end-to-end, meaning only you can access your content. We never read your journal entries, and your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

Can I export my journal entries?

Yes, you can export all your journal entries at any time in multiple formats, including PDF and plain text. Your data always belongs to you, and you can download it or transfer it whenever you wish.

What makes Memora different from other journaling apps?

Memora stands out with its thoughtful AI that provides personalized insights rather than generic feedback. Our mood tracking gives you valuable patterns over time, and our intuitive design makes journaling feel effortless. We've focused on creating an experience that helps you develop meaningful self-reflection.
